Quality

Fruits and vegetables should be attractive, fresh, nutritive and with a good appearance and presentation. Besides these characteristics, their consumption should not put health at risk. The consumer has no way to detect the presence of dangerous substances on food and he/she depends entirely on the seriousness and responsibility of all the members of the production and distribution chain. Necessarily, he/she has to trust them, in addition to the usual precautions of washing, peeling and/or cooking the product before consuming it. However, this trust is very volatile and any suspicion about safety has a tremendous impact at consumer level.
